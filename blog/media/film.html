moonlight (2016) dir. barry jerkins
i was 15 when this film released (insane to think about). this was like a tight hug around my teenage
self as a black, queer person – being raised around homophobia/transphobia, not having a genuine
support system at home, and being bullied simply for being who i am. this film truly made me feel seen.
amazing performances all around. ‘moonlight’ will always have a special place in my heart, and i’m so
happy that i was able to experience, feel, and understand it at a time when i needed it.
